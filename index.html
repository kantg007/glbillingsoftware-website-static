About GL Billing Software

GL Billing Software is India's first free billing software designed for Indian businesses. It helps retailers, wholesalers, timber traders, hardware stores, electronics stores, grocery stores and other businesses manage GST billing, inventory and accounting from a single platform.


Features include GST invoice creation, inventory management, customer ledger, supplier ledger, purchase management, sale management, stock reports, profit and loss reports, GSTR reports and professional invoice printing.


GL Billing Software is especially useful for timber traders because it supports lot management, CBM, CFT calculations and timber-specific workflows. The software is designed to simplify daily business operations while remaining completely free to use.
